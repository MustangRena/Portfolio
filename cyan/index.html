About Me

Hi, I'm NaRena--a web developer with a passion for building websites that blend functionality and design to help small businesses thrive online. My journey into tech started after a career in logistics, and I've never looked back.

When I'm not coding, I'm immersed in music, learning to play the violin and exploring its ability to inspire creativity. Music has taught me patience, focus, and the beauty of continuous growth--qualities I bring to every project. I also enjoy wandering through Chattanooga's picturesque Riverparks, and finding peace in mountain vistas.
